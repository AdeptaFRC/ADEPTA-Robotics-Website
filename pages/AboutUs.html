Foundation

ADEPTA was founded in 2024 at Ort Afridar Ashqelon High School. The team competes in the FRC - FIRST Robotics Competition.

Members

The Israeli team members that are 10-12th grade Robotics students from Ortt Afridar Ashqelon High School.

Competitions

Since its foundation in 2005, ORBIT 1690 has competed in 12 Israeli regionals, 2 regionals in the United States, and had competed in the World Championship 9 times, with 1 Einstein Finals appearance.
